Basket Catching Game

Welcome to the basket catching game! In this game your objective is to catch as many falling objects as you can with your trusty basket. Press the left and right arrow keys to move your basket sideways, and up and down to move it vertically. You'll have to be quick - you only have 12 seconds to catch as much as possible. Try to beat the current high score!
